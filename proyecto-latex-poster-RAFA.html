Project Overview

This project showcases a scientific poster presented at the RAFA (Reunión Anual de la Asociación Física Argentina) conference. The research focuses on complex physical phenomena, requiring precise data visualization and academic formatting. This was the result of over six months of work, involving the development of several Python scripts. These were used not only to operate complex and delicate instruments but also for the subsequent data processing, where cleaning and analysis were automated to achieve an accurate characterization of the equipment.

The entire document was typeset using LaTeX, ensuring high-quality mathematical notation and professional layout standards.
