Project Overview

This project explores the fascinating intersection of physical sciences and financial data modeling. By treating banking transactions and queuing systems as particles in a closed system, we can apply the principles of statistical mechanics and the ideal gas law to predict dynamic banking behaviors. The entire simulation, data generation, and visualization were programmed using Python.
